## 1. Equipment Sabotage
**Threat:** Intentional damage to production equipment

**Mitigating Controls:**
- Card Access System
- CCTV System - IP/Network
- Background Checks
- Machine Guarding & Safety Interlocks

**Survey Questions:**
1. **Production Access Control**
   - Q: "Is access to production floor and critical equipment restricted to authorized personnel?"

   - Evidence: Photo of access readers
   - Control: Card Access System

   - Type: Yes/No

   - Control: CCTV System - IP/Network

3. **Equipment Protection**
